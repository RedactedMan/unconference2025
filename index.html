Evolution of an App

Tathagata Dasgupta

Change is one of the core values of LSEG. As developers, we live it everyday - between the git commits, continuous integration and continuous delivery - a lot of our craft is about harnessing change and promising stability to our clients. What we have learned is that small doses over a cadence, is more effective over a big bang. Make it work, make it right, make it better - this talk will try to distill the lessons learned while migrating an app from a single node deployment to a distributed solution, and how we took the opportunity to evolve it using strategic LSEG tools.
